Noé Cabannes Michel

President Publishing director Founder

Noé Cabannes Michel is a strong advocate of access to education and of social inclusivity in reading, especially for kids and adults that show signs of Attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD). In 2025, he founded Art x Science International, an NGO that aims to adapt pubic-domain books designed for maximum reading retention thanks to AI made by teachers and recent studies in the ADHD scope.
